Warehouse Navigating Robot

For the final portfolio of Embedded System and Design course, we built a prototype for a warehouse navigating robot that is controlled by voice commands through Amazon Dot(Alexa). The robot navigates through the warehouse using color sensors to store or fetch any item.It also maintains a list of item that's already stored in the inventory so it will not go fetch a product that is out of stock.

Tvent (Android Application)

Team SmartOne is developing an android application called Tvent. Our goal is to educate all the users about their surroundings. This application will send notifications to its users about the popular events happening around them. Tvent gets its data from twitter feeds, therefore, it will not notify the users about all the public events happening around them. Instead, it will notify its user about the events that most people are tweeting about. Since we do not limit our events to be of some specific kind, our application will be used by a wide number of users. Some benefits that our application provides to its users are: information about events that are not broadcasted in news channels, trending topics that will help travellers to familiarize themselves with the culture that is followed in that particular area.
